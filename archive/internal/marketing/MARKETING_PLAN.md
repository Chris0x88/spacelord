Drive awareness and adoption of Pacman as the primary "Agentic Primitive" for the Hedera ecosystem.

- **The "Strategy Tweakers"**: Users who want to build/chop/change trade logic in simple .md files and let AI loops handle the execution.
- **The "Ghost Walkers"**: Hedera enthusiasts who know the chain is quiet but see the cool potential of its fixed-fee/high-speed infra.
